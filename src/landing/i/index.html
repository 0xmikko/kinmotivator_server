Feature Highlights

Set up tasks by voice

To set tasks up is just to press button repeat task and responsible persons loud. Application recognises your voice, add task to the list and set responsible persons correctly.

Copy tasks to Wrike in one click

When you meeting is over, just press Finish button and all tasks will be copied to selected folder in your Wrike account. Plan tasks in WrikeMeeting, control them in Wrike.

Plan your meetings with your team

Select your team mates from list and your meeting automatically appears on their devices. During the meeting your collegues could join it and see / change tasks list online.

Analyze your meetings

WrikeMeeting monitors time your spent for each meeting, tasks were set up and percent of completed in time. Use this analytics to improve meetings quality.

Learn, play and discuss

Learn new by reading selected posts, improve your skills, earn reward points by setting tasks efficiently and compete with others.
